Associate Proffesor

I am an associate professor at the Department of Botany, Palacký University Olomouc (Czech Republic) and chairman of the Czech Phycological Society. I was a postdoc at Uppsala University (Sweden) and completed habilitation at Charles University, Prague (Czech Republic). I am interested in evolutionary biology, taxonomy, and systematics of cyanobacteria and algae. Currently, I am investigating factors driving the emergence of new species in Microcoleus (cyanobacteria) and chrysophytes (Synura and Mallomonas) using population genomics, phylogenomics, and bioinformatics. I also pursue the application of phylogenomics and population genomics into the taxonomy across all cyanobacterial groups.

Researchers

Mgr. Aleksandar Stanojković, Ph.D.

I am an evolutionary biologist interested in genomics of microorganisms, especially cyanobacteria. I completed my PhD at Palacký University Olomouc in 2023, where I investigated evolutionary forces governing diversification of cyanobacteria on a global and local scale by focusing on genome-wide signatures of diversity and divergence. I am a postdoctoral researcher in the same lab since October 2023. I'm currently applying bioinfomatics approaches to study the adaptation and genetic variation of Microcoleus cyanobacterium using nanopore sequencing data.
